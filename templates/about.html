Project Intuition

This webapp is made due to the fact that we don't have the technology and knowledge to incorporate Mediapipe into our Flutter application. However, we have already trained a model for most of the ASL alphabets and we do not want let our effor in vain. Hence, we decided to build this web app and host our ML model here as a prove of our effort.


Limitations

Despite the remarkable performance of the current model, the model still can't recognize ASL gesture with sequences. For example, English alphabet J and Z. This is due to the fact that our datasets are only static image with labelled landmarks. In the future, we will enhance this model and make it capable to recognize ASL with sequences.
